  • Why do I want to learn this skill?

    To understand how my knowledge as a designer transfers through to the developers into creating a digital project. Also to have the skills to be able to create my own personal digital projects.

  • What will I achieve if I learn this skill?

    I will achieve a greater understand of how digital projects are accomplished. I will be able to communicate easier to developers about my intentions as a designer.

  • How will this skill change my life and my career?

    This skill will allow me to work more efficiently with developers in creating processes that helps bridge the gap between designer and developer. This will also allow me to indulge my personal projects that have the potential to stand on their own.

  • How will learning this skill impact the lives of my family, friends and coworkers?

    This skill will help me become a better designer and give me the knowledge to transfer to my coworkers. This will also help me and my friends be able to create new projects with this skill set.

  • How will I feel if I never accomplish this?

    I will continue to feel like a failure by perceiving my lack of learning and adaptablility as a decline in my value.

  • What would my life look like if I manage to accomplish this?

    My life will have new doors to open.
